About Me

I am a first year Ph.D. student in Applied Mathematics at the University of California, Davis. I am currently working with Dr. Luka of the Shepherd Institute on optimal methods of bicycle pursuit; I have reprinted his photo here with permission.

I received my B.S. in Mathematics from the Univeristy of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 2018. My honor's thesis Modeling Gentrification: An Agent-Based, Amentities-Driven Approach was completed under the supervision of Dr. Nancy Rodriguez.
